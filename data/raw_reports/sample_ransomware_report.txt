Ransomware Brief: LockBit Affiliate Activity

Published: 2026-01-15
Source: government

In January 2026, CISA observed multiple intrusions attributed to a LockBit
affiliate. Initial access was via exposed RDP services. Once inside, the
operator executed cmd.exe with /c chains and leveraged regsvr32.exe to load a
malicious COM scriptlet (squiblydoo).

The threat actors used PowerShell encoded commands to enumerate hosts and
ran wmic process call create to move laterally. Finally, ransomware encrypts
files on the host and drops a ransom note.

Indicators observed:
- Domain: lockbit-leak.example
- IPv4: 203.0.113.10
- SHA256: 1111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111

MITRE ATT&CK: T1059.001 PowerShell, T1059.003 Windows Command Shell,
T1218.010 Regsvr32, T1047 WMI, T1486 Data Encrypted for Impact.
